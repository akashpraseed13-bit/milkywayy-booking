# Product Guidelines - Milkywayy Booking

## Visual Identity
- **Theme:** "Futuristic Professionalism." A sophisticated blend of dark, celestial aesthetics with clean, minimalist usability.
- **Backgrounds:** Utilize subtle space and star motifs (e.g., `StarBackground`, `SpaceBackground`) to establish a unique identity without compromising readability.
- **Color Palette:** Primarily dark mode (deep blues/blacks) with high-contrast, professional accents. Avoid overly playful or chaotic colors; stick to a refined, cohesive scheme.
- **Typography:**
    - **Primary Font:** **Poppins** for all body text, UI elements, and navigation to ensure maximum readability and a modern, clean feel.
    - **Accent Font:** **Michroma** reserved strictly for rare, high-impact use cases (branding/headers) or deprecated if not essential.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Tone:** Futuristic, innovative, and deeply user-centric. The interface should feel advanced yet intuitive, guiding users effortlessly through tasks.
- **Interaction:** Smooth, polished transitions and clearly defined interactive states.
    - **Photography Hover:** Items in the "Photography" category utilize a smooth grayscale-to-color transition on desktop to emphasize visual clarity upon interaction.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast between text and backgrounds, especially within the dark theme.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity First:** While the aesthetic is "space-themed," the primary goal is functional booking. Design elements should never obscure content or functionality.
- **Consistent Layouts:** Maintain uniform spacing and component structure (using the existing `ui` components) across Admin and Customer views.
