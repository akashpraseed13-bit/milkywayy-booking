# Product Guide - Milkywayy Booking

## Initial Concept
Milkywayy Booking is a comprehensive booking and management platform designed to provide a seamless scheduling experience for customers while offering robust administrative tools for business management.

## Target Users
1. **Customers/Clients (Primary):** Individuals seeking to book services, manage their appointments, and access related documents/invoices.
2. **Administrators (Secondary):** System owners or managers who oversee bookings, user accounts, pricing, and promotional content.
3. **Staff/Employees (Future):** Personnel who will handle day-to-day operations and service delivery.

## Core Goals
- **Seamless Booking Experience:** To provide an intuitive and frictionless process for customers to discover and schedule services.
- **Administrative Efficiency:** To automate and streamline management tasks such as invoicing, user management, and dynamic pricing.
- **Secure Financial Operations:** To ensure safe and reliable payment processing through integrated services.

## Key Features
- **Interactive Booking Flow:** A user-friendly calendar interface for selecting dates and time slots, integrated with real-time availability.
- **Customer Dashboard:** A centralized portal for users to view their booking history, download invoices, and manage their profile.
- **Admin Management Suite:** A comprehensive control panel for managing users, configuring discounts/coupons, adjusting pricings, and overseeing system status.
- **Payment Integration:** Secure checkout process leveraging Stripe for transaction handling.
